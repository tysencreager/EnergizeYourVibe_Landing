The 7 Pillars Framework

The heart behind the 7 Pillars.

Hey Sister,

While this community was built on connection, friendship, and fun, everything we do inside is rooted in the 7 Pillars Framework.

The 7 Pillars aren't a curriculum or a checklist to complete. They're simply seven important areas of life that we'll keep coming back to because they all play a role in how we feel and how we live.

ALIGN — Spiritual Wellness
FEEL — Emotional Wellness
THINK — Mindset & Mental Clarity
FUEL — Physical Health & Energy
CONNECT — Relationships & Community
FLOW — Money, Lifestyle & Receiving
SHINE — Purpose, Expression & Confidence

The truth is, most of us are doing really well in a few areas while quietly running on low or empty in others. That's normal. We're human.

Life has seasons. Sometimes family, our health, emotions, or relationships can end up taking a back seat for a while. That's one of the reasons this sisterhood exists. You don't have to figure it all out on your own.

Each month, you can focus on one of the pillars through a live call with me and a coach or guest expert who can offer practical tools, encouragement, and fresh perspective. You'll also find resources & conversations had throughout the month in the FB community, lives for vibes & the library — to help you stay connected and keep moving forward.

There's no pressure to work on everything at once. Just one step. One choice. One day at a time. Give yourself the same grace you would offer a friend.

We're here to encourage you, celebrate your wins, and remind you that you don't have to do life alone.

The goal isn't to be perfect in all seven pillars. It's to become more aware of where you are, celebrate what's going well, and gently give attention to the areas that could use a little more support.

I'm so glad you're here. You can find the 7 Pillars call schedule below. While showing up on the Zoom calls in person will give you the best value, the calls will be recorded and sit in the library for 30 days after, so you can access them when your time allows you to engage and listen with intention.
