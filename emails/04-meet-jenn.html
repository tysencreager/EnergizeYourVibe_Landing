Meet Jenn

The story behind Energize Your Vibe.

Hey Sister,

I want to get vulnerable and share a little more about why this community exists and why Energize Your Vibe™ means so much to me.

I was widowed at 22 years old with a young child and found myself standing in the middle of a life I never planned. I felt lost, alone, and buried in grief.

I went to therapy. I took antidepressants to help aid. I did my best to survive.

Then one day, something inside me said, there has got to be more to life than feeling this way. You can either sink or swim.

I made the choice to keep going.

That choice led me down a path of rebuilding my life. Over the years, I immersed myself in developing self, reading books, coaching, seminars, therapy, healing work, nervous system support, and big faith. I wanted more than just getting by. I wanted to continue to create a life I truly loved living.

Life didn't stop with the challenges.

Along the way, I've lost two of my childhood best friends to cancer. (Oh, how I miss my Amers and Leesy Girl.) I've been impacted by loss of loved ones to suicide, walked beside people battling addiction and mental health struggles, as well as facing my own grief and mental health challenges.

I look back and can see what a victim I was being at times. How the things that happened to me were creating false truths inside of me. How every time something happened, I was reacting from the past & dealing with an unregulated nervous system. Boy does it feel good to live in such PEACE around everything. It's ok how I felt, how I was and how I operated — until I didn't. It was deep, hard, and very dark at times.

I'm not here because it was easy. I'm here because I chose myself along the way.

I know what it feels like to lose yourself. And, I know what it takes to slowly find your way back.

One of the things I've learned is that when life gets hard, it's easy to isolate. Shame, guilt, embarrassment, fear... they can quietly convince us to pull away from the very people who could help us feel less alone.

That's one of the reasons I created Energize Your Vibe™. Not because we need to know everyone's deepest struggles. Because we all need a place where we can simply come and be as we are.

Some days you'll come because you need encouragement. Some days you'll come because you want to laugh, connect, and have fun. And maybe you're in a season where life is really good. I love that! We want to celebrate that, too.

Life isn't only about making it through the hard seasons. It's meant to be lived!

The Energize Your Vibe™ community, 7 Pillars Framework, and the Energize Your Vibe Method™ have been on my heart for years. This community was inspired straight from a creator who sees us. It has been built from my own experiences & inspiration, education, science, and tools that work. Looking back, I truly believe God was preparing me for this work long before I knew what it would become. I knew from a young age that I was called to experience things that would help me not just understand people, but relate to how they feel to be able to offer compassion & empathy.

Today, I bring together my experience and training in business, coaching, holistic healing, applied kinesiology, energy work, yoga, meditation, sound healing, and ICF coaching to create a community that supports the whole person... body, mind, and spirit.

More than anything, I hope you feel loved here. I hope you feel seen. I hope you feel encouraged. And I hope this becomes a place where you can be yourself, continue growing, and build meaningful friendships along the way.

I'm so grateful you're here, and I can't wait to get to know your story too.
